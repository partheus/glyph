colophon

What is this?

Colophon (n): A statement at the end of a book or document providing information about its production, such as the publication date, authorship, and printing details.

Why is this?

This site came about as a direct result of a small train of thought I let my mind loose on; on a Monday of all days, Jeeves, my goodness.

Pah.

What we have here is simply a smaller, simpler domain for me to pour a beaker or two of energy into.

The quote on the main page is by Terry Pratchett, one of my favourite authors.

Tech

The site is, of course, built via Eleventy and hosted on Github Pages.

The monospace font stack is as follows:

Menlo, Consolas, Monaco, Liberation Mono, Lucida Console, Andale Mono WT, Andale Mono, Lucida Sans Typewriter, DejaVu Sans Mono, Bitstream Vera Sans Mono, Nimbus Mono L, Courier, monospace;

Design

The design and layout of this site is directly inspired by many personal sites of the older internet.

I am a little young, but I am fond of the older web. And there are many sites like it, but this one is mine.

There is a light and a dark theme that switches per the user's browser/system preference.
